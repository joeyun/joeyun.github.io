Web Designs

IBM Landing Page

After looking at the cloud computing landing page of IBM’s website, I thought I’d take the time to simply redesign their page to be more visually appealing and interesting for users by taking a more modern and minimalism approach. Using current design trends like vivid colors, gradients, high contrast, minimalism designs/icons, and lots of white space, I put together this new design for IBM.

Sign-up Page

There are countless web pages on the internet that have a sign-up page. There are many variations and styles of the sign-up page and I’ve found many to be a bit too complicated, long or just unappealing to look at. This is a user interface I designed for a clean and minimalist sign-up page. It simply asks you for your name, email, and desired password.

Checkout Page

This design splits the screen into two sections and allows users to see the items in their car that they are checking out. It provides the users with a rotatable gallery of the items in the cart along with the individual and total prices of the items on the bottom left. On the right side, the users can enter their payment information.

Goten of Japan

Goten of Japan is a Japanese hibachi restaurant. I worked here for several years and noticed that their website was a bit out-dated. I incorporated their desired white and black theme while keeping the overall site easy to navigate and clean. View here!

EnviroFacts App


About the app

Envirofacts is an app concept that aims to educate its users about the environment and help spread awareness on topics like climate change and pollution. It incorporates gamification and sharing features all tied together by clean minimalist designs.
